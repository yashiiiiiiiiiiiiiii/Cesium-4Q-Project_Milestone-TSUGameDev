Summer Memories

This is a visual novel on your final thirty days of summer vacation before the decisive entrance exams. You have met someone you deem your true love, and it is your job is to establish a relationship with her.

How to Play

Step 1: Enter your name and click Start to begin your 30-day journey.

Step 2:Read the story text in the navy box at the bottom.

Step 3: Click the orange choice buttons to make decisions. Decisions increase your Points with Yonna.

Goal: Reach the end of summer with the highest Points possible to see the best ending!
